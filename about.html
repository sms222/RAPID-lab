Who we are

RAPID is a collaborative research group based at the Centre for Quality Management of Medicines, Faculty of Pharmacy, Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia (UKM).

We work closely with clinicians and researchers to improve drug dosing using applied pharmacokinetics, pharmacometrics, and pharmacogenetics, with strong emphasis on real-world clinical data and assay-linked research.

Core research strengths

  • Population pharmacokinetics and PKPD modelling
  • Therapeutic drug monitoring (TDM)
  • Pharmacogenetics and pharmacogenomics
  • Precision dosing in special populations
  • Assay-linked translational studies
