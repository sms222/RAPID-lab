Work with RAPID

RAPID provides expertise in pharmacokinetics, pharmacometrics, and clinical pharmacology to support clinically relevant and translational research.

We work closely with clinicians, researchers, and postgraduate students to design studies, analyse data, and generate evidence that informs real-world dosing decisions.
