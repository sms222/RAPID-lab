RAPID provides expertise in pharmacokinetics, pharmacometrics, and clinical pharmacology to support clinical and translational research.

We welcome collaborations in

  • Study design and protocol development
  • Population PK / PKPD modelling and simulation
  • Therapeutic drug monitoring (TDM)
  • Grant applications and methodology support

Methods & platforms

  • NONMEM, Monolix, MATLAB
  • HPLC and LC-MS/MS analytical methods
  • Coagulation assays (where relevant)

Typical collaborators

  • Clinicians and clinical researchers
  • Hospital research units
  • Postgraduate students
  • Industry partners
